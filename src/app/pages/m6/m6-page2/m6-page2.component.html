SIP Process Overview-Administration

SIP administration is an on-going process that ensures that plans are running as intended.
  • Sales compensation plans are typically administrated with the aid of tools that help automate the process
  • Plans should be setup in the system
  • Best practice would be for plan participants to receive a statement explaining their payout concurrently with receiving the payout

Preparing Effective Plan Administration

First Decision: Where will administration be managed?
  • Centralized Team supporting Business?
  • Multiple Teams supporting major Departments?
  • Decentralized Resources supporting teams
Elements of a Plan Administration Plan:
  • Resources Needed for Administration
  • Sales Plan Measure Data
  • Eligible Population Data
  • Incentive Funding Calculations
  • Award Recommendation Process (if needed)
  • Award Review and Approvals
  • Payroll Processing
