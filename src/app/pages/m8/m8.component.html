SIP implementation helps to minimize the complications and pitfalls of the previous system while improving plan transparency, seller motivation, and metric tracking.
Old vs. New
What was working before SIP: Performance measures executed within a shorter timeframe. These allow sellers to focus on execution, which in turn makes the measures more comprehensible and achievable. What was not working: Alignment around performance metrics including: pay thresholds, and excellence points, especially across regions and countries. Sales leaders and sellers both have had difficulty understanding how current incentives work and how they can be leveraged.
Moving Forward Key Metrics
New opportunity to highlight key areas where growth opportunities are most lucrative, improve sales morale and behavior, and simplify the understanding of connecting day-to-day work with total compensation. Plan designs function and are funded depending on the complexities of customer administration and seller type. The Sprint process will greatly improve the understanding and visibility of investment vs. performance for sellers.
