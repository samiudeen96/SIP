SIP Process Overview-Design

The SIP Design process helps develop best-in-class plans that drive desired behaviors and align with team and business unit strategy.

Various stakeholders will be involved in the design process

  • Roles will vary from executing the actual design, having approval rights and providing feedback and input

Once all roles are mapped to a platform job, design sales compensation on a platform role basis

Review best practices for each platform role

  • What are examples of compensation plans for each role?

Design will need to ensure that all measures are quantifiable and trackable

Compensation costing will need to be run to ensure that plans are in line with budget

Expected SIP Timeline (High-Level)

The SIP Process should initiate in Q1 and be finalized before the end of the fiscal year to ensure successful rollout for the upcoming fiscal year.

Key Design Questions To Address

Design Topic Key Design Questions
General
  • How many plan “types” do we design for?
  • How much customization / variation do we allow to account for territory differences?
Measures
  • How should new customers/accounts be measured?
  • How do we account for different territory growth rates, new logo opportunities, and run-rate business levels
Pay Curve
  • What are the appropriate below 100% attainment pay curve breakpoints?
  • What are the appropriate corresponding acceleration rates
  • Do pay curves differ between sellers in different territories?
Performance Period
  • What is the appropriate performance period?
  • What is the ideal calculation method of performance (discrete, cumulative, YTD cumulative, etc.)
Policies
  • What is the account transition policy from new logo sellers? Do they retain the account permanently?
