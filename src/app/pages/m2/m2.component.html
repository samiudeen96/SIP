Manage

Plan

  1. Alignment of business strategy, GTM approach, sales coverage and organization design decisions
  2. Positions defined consistently with clear accountability and behaviors

Assess

  1. Collect sales force feedback
  2. Analyze pay-for-performance, effectiveness
  3. Compare plan results to appropriate market data (pay and practices)
  4. Ongoing sales performance and operations reporting is performed

Design

  1. Align with compensation design principles
  2. Clear line of sight between measures and outcomes
  3. Measures are feasible
  4. Plans are competitive with external market
  5. Design includes cross functional input

Administer

  1. Systems track and report on progress, metrics
  2. Mid-year crediting or sales target adjustments reviewed through governance
  3. Employee results communication and payment processing

Implement

  1. Plans, targets and territories determined prior to new fiscal year
  2. Legal reviews
  3. Administration system setup
  4. Change and Communications Management
  5. Governance
