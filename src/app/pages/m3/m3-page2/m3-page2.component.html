Design Team Selection Process

How to think about organizing the team.

When pulling together a design team to ultimately recommend a sales compensation design to your Steering Committee, you should choose members that will:

  • Provide meaningful expertise to the design process
  • Represent resources needed for meaningful compensation plan design and implementation
  • Lend credibility to the proposed solution with the Steering Committee
  • Act as change agents later on in the implementation process
  • Have the capacity to make the needed time commitment

Design Team Selection Process – Org Structure

Typically, design teams are comprised of 7-8 members representing the following expertise areas. Someone should be identified as the overall initiative owner.
MD (recommended)

Informs strategic alignment, voice of steering committee and owns outcome

Sales Management

Brings field perspective, confirms current practices

Finance

Provides performance data, validates cost/benefit

Human Resources

Anticipates change impact, provides market data

Project Manager

Owns project execution, clarifies objectives, oversees day-to-day operations

Sales Operations

Reviews job content, provides data, collaborating during design process

Other

Consider representation from IT, Marketing

Steering Committee Selection Process

How a well-organized Steering Committee Contributes.

The Steering Committee should serve as the ultimate leadership team during the SIP transformation process. Their overarching purpose is to:

  • Set the business, sales strategy and financial objectives
  • Define sales compensation design principles and next fiscal year’s sales compensation goals
  • Approve plan design, costs and implementation plan
  • Resolve escalated issues from Design Team, including issues and conflicts across organizations
