Pay for Performance Philosophy

A key step in the SIP process is developing the overall pay-for-performance philosophy. This philosophy will dictate key SIP design elements, such as plan structure, pay mix, and measures. The key decision point at this stage will be where we want to be in relation to the market, and how much we want to differentiate pay for low performers versus high performers.
