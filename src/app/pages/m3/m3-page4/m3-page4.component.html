Deciding What Roles Will Be Part of SIP Design

Selecting the roles that will be part of the SIP design process is a crucial planning step.

Selected roles should be directly responsible for driving sales or supporting sales

Typical roles that are under SIP include:

  • Account Managers
  • Sales Professionals
  • Sales Managers
  • National Sales Managers

Once roles are selected, they are grouped into Platform Jobs based on the nature of work being perfomed

What is a Platform Job?

A grouping of jobs performing similar duties based on its strategy, specialization, sales process, route-to-market, segmentation, and job type.
Replaces HR Job Profile titles.
Not defined by organizational structure, pay level, or product, account, or partner specific assignments (unless it results in a unique attribute).
Does not replace Business Titles.
